# IT Guidelines

## Laptop Issuance
All full-time employees will be issued a company laptop within 3 days of joining. Temporary staff may use shared systems.

## Password Policy
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Minimum 12 characters
- Use at least one uppercase, one number, and one symbol

## Software Installation
Unauthorized software installations are not allowed. Employees must request approvals via the IT Request Form.

## VPN Usage
Remote access to internal systems must be done through the company VPN. Credentials will be sent during onboarding.

## Support Hours
IT support is available from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday to Friday. Email: it-help@company.io
