Code of Conduct
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Purpose: To promote a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace environment.

Expectations:
- Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect, regardless of race, gender, religion, or other characteristics.
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to HR immediately.
- Use company resources (e.g., equipment, funds) responsibly and for work purposes only.

Prohibited Behaviors:
- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination in any form.
- Unauthorized sharing of proprietary information.
- Engaging in personal activities during work hours that interfere with job responsibilities.

Reporting Violations:
- Report concerns to HR or use the anonymous reporting hotline at (800) 555-1234.
- Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

Consequences: Violations may lead to warnings, suspension, or termination, depending on severity.
